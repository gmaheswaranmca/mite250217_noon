`5. Swapping Two Numbers (Using Call by Reference)`
`Problem Statement:`  
Write a program to swap two numbers 
using `call by reference` (pointers).  

`Function Prototype:`  
```c
void swap(int *a, int *b);
```

`Sample Test Case:`  
`Input:`  
```plaintext
Enter two numbers: 5 10  
```
`Output:`  
```plaintext
After Swapping:  
First Number: 10  
Second Number: 5  
```
